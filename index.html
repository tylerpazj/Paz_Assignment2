Oh SNAP! Bedford-Stuyvesant Residents are Relying on Governmental Assistance to Combat Hunger

Data from NYC's Human Resources Administration shows a monthly rise in SNAP recipients in Bed-Stuy.

By Tyler Paz

November 23, 2024

Bedford-Stuyvesant has a higher than city-rate of residents who utilize the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program, more commonly known as SNAP. SNAP recipients use the Electronic Benefit Transfer, a system that allows participants to purchase food. Pulling numbers from New York City Open Data shows that about a third of all residents are enrolled in the aforementioned programs. In fact, between Sept. 2023 to Sept. 2024, the number of households that received SNAP consultations grew by approximately 4%.
